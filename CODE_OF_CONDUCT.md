# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want `l40s-llm-bench` to be a practical, respectful place to improve
reproducible LLM inference benchmark tooling. We welcome participation from
people with different backgrounds, hardware access, serving stacks, and levels
of benchmark experience.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume measurement mistakes are possible, including your own.
- Distinguish evidence, interpretation, and speculation.
- Help make benchmark reports reproducible rather than performative.
- Give credit for ideas, tests, reports, and fixes.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Misrepresenting benchmark results, hardware access, or affiliations.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors to accept unsupported claims.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit
participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a conduct concern, contact the maintainers through the
security contact path if the matter is private, or open an issue if it can be
discussed publicly. Maintainers will handle reports with appropriate
confidentiality and proportionality.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from common open source project norms and is
intended to keep the project usable for careful, evidence-based collaboration.
