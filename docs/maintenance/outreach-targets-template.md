# Outreach Targets Template

This is a blank tracking template for real outreach only. Do not prefill it
with invented people, assumed reactions, or feedback that has not been
received.

Use one row per real person or community contact after you decide to contact
them. Keep private email addresses and sensitive notes out of public issues.

## Scope

- Ask for dry-run and fake-server smoke-test feedback only.
- Do not ask for GPU time, real model serving, or public benchmark numbers.
- Do not record silence as feedback.
- Do not imply that a person endorsed the project unless they explicitly said
  so.

## Short English Outreach

Hi [name], I am collecting early usability feedback on `l40s-llm-bench`. Would
you be willing to try the dry-run and local fake-server smoke test? It takes
about 10 to 15 minutes, does not require a GPU, and should not include any real
benchmark numbers or private endpoint details. The guide is
`docs/first-user-smoke-test.md`.

## Short Chinese Outreach

## After Feedback Arrives

Record only what was actually received:

- link to the issue or private note location
- concrete failure or confusion
- environment details the tester chose to share
- any redaction or privacy constraints
- whether a maintainer follow-up is needed
